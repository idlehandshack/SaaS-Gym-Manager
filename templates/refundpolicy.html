The short version: You get a free trial before paying anything. Postpaid users can cancel anytime with no fees. First-time prepaid subscribers can request a refund within 7 days. If EnterGYM breaks something we promised — we'll fix it or refund you.

1

Our Commitment to You

At EnterGYM, we want you to feel completely confident before you spend a single rupee. That's why every new customer gets a risk-free evaluation period:

Launchpad
3 months of full Premium+ access at zero cost — or until 50 active members, whichever comes first. No credit card required.
Postpaid & Prepaid plans
Both include a 1-month free trial on first signup. No charges during the trial period.

We encourage you to use these free periods thoroughly. They exist so you can evaluate EnterGYM completely — with real members, real data — before any money changes hands.

2

Postpaid Plans

Postpaid subscribers are billed at the end of each month based on actual usage. Here's how cancellations and refunds work:

Cancel Anytime
No lock-in. Cancel before your next billing cycle and you won't be charged again.
No Cancellation Fees
Zero penalties, zero exit charges. Cancellation is always free.
No Partial Refunds
We don't issue refunds for days already used in a billing month.
Data Access
After cancellation, your data remains accessible for 30 days for export.
3

Prepaid Plans

Prepaid plans are paid upfront for quarterly or annual periods at a discounted rate. The following refund rules apply:

Scenario Eligible? Details
First prepaid payment, within 7 days YES Full refund, no questions asked. Contact us within 7 calendar days of payment.
First prepaid payment, after 7 days NO Outside the guarantee window. We can issue a service credit for remaining months instead.
Renewal payments NO Renewal payments are non-refundable. Cancel before the renewal date to avoid charges.
Service outage caused by us YES Pro-rated credit or refund for downtime exceeding our 99.5% SLA.

To request a refund, email us at support@entergym.in with your gym ID and payment reference. We process eligible refunds within 5–7 business days.

4

WhatsApp Costs

WhatsApp Business API messages are billed by Meta at per-conversation rates and passed through to you at cost. These charges are separate from your EnterGYM subscription.

WhatsApp conversation credits are consumed when messages are sent and cannot be refunded once delivered. Unused credits may roll over within the same billing month but are not refundable upon cancellation.

If a message fails to deliver due to a platform error on our side (not Meta's network), we will credit the failed conversation charge back to your account automatically within 48 hours.

5

Our Promise to You

If EnterGYM fails to deliver on a feature or service we explicitly promised in writing, we commit to the following resolution path:

Step 1 — Fix It First
We'll resolve the issue within a reasonable timeframe, usually 48–72 hours for critical bugs.
Step 2 — Service Credit
If we can't fix it, we'll issue a prorated credit covering the affected period.
Step 3 — Full Refund
If the credit isn't satisfactory and we've genuinely failed our commitment, we'll refund the affected billing period in full.
6

Plan Changes

Upgrading your plan takes effect immediately. The difference is billed on a prorated basis for the remainder of the current period.

Downgrading takes effect at the start of the next billing period. No refunds are issued for the difference on the current period, but you retain access to higher-tier features until then.

Switching from Prepaid to Postpaid (or vice versa) takes effect at the end of your current prepaid term. Contact support to schedule the change.

7

Free Trial

Every new gym gets a 1-month free trial on their first plan subscription. During this period:

Zero charges
No payment method required during trial. You won't be billed until the trial ends.
Full access
All Premium+ features unlocked. No artificial restrictions during evaluation.

Gyms that joined under the Launchpad program receive 3 months free (or up to 50 active members). This is separate from the standard 1-month trial and applies automatically at signup.

8

Policy Updates

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll notify active subscribers via email and in-app notification at least 14 days before any changes take effect.

Continuing to use EnterGYM after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. If you disagree with material changes, you may cancel your subscription before they take effect and request a prorated refund for the remaining period.

9

Contact Us

Have a question about a charge or want to request a refund? We respond to all refund-related queries within 1 business day.

Get in Touch
