How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect only to deliver and improve EnterGYM. Specifically:

  • To provision and operate your gym's isolated subdomain and dashboard
  • To process membership payments and generate GST-compliant invoices
  • To verify GPS-based attendance within your gym's geofenced radius
  • To power AI Face Attendance recognition at your gym entrance
  • To send push notifications and WhatsApp reminders on your behalf to members
  • To generate monthly attendance, revenue, and member analytics reports
  • To provide customer support and respond to your queries
  • To detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, abuse, or security incidents
  • To comply with applicable Indian laws and regulatory requirements
We do not: sell your data, use your member data for advertising, share gym analytics across tenants, or process face data for any purpose other than attendance at your specific gym.
4

Your Member Data

As a gym owner using EnterGYM, you are the data controller for your gym members' personal information. EnterGYM acts as a data processor — we store and process member data only on your instructions.

  • All member data is stored in your gym's fully isolated database
  • No other gym on the EnterGYM platform can access your members' information
  • You are responsible for obtaining consent from your members before enrolling them
  • You may export all member data as a CSV or PDF report at any time
  • We do not use your members' data for any purpose outside running your gym
Biometric Data (AI Face Attendance): Face encodings are computed locally, stored only in your gym's isolated environment, and are never transferred to other gyms, third parties, or used for profiling. Gym owners must obtain explicit written consent from members before enrolling them in face attendance.
5

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Infrastructure providers: Our VPS host and Cloudflare for CDN/DNS — bound by data processing agreements
  • Cloud storage: Cloudflare R2 for encrypted database backups — data is encrypted before upload
  • Payment gateways: To process subscription payments — they never receive your member data
  • Push notification services: FCM (Google Firebase) for delivering push alerts — only device tokens, no personal data
  • Legal compliance: If required by Indian law, court order, or government authority
We never share your gym's member profiles, attendance records, payment history, face data, or business analytics with any third party for advertising, research, or commercial purposes.

Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your gym's data:

🔐
Encryption in TransitAll traffic encrypted via TLS 1.3 / HTTPS with wildcard Let's Encrypt certificates
🛡️
Cloudflare ProtectionDDoS mitigation, WAF rules, and DNS proxying for all subdomains
🗄️
Encrypted BackupsAutomated PostgreSQL backups encrypted and stored on Cloudflare R2
🔑
Secure SessionsDjango sessions with HttpOnly, Secure, and SameSite cookie flags
🏠
Tenant IsolationEach gym's database is fully isolated — cross-gym access is architecturally impossible

If you discover a security vulnerability in EnterGYM, please report it responsibly to support@entergym.in. We aim to acknowledge reports within 24 hours.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your EnterGYM subscription is active, plus a grace period after termination:

  • Active subscription: All data retained indefinitely while your account is active
  • After cancellation: Data retained for 90 days to allow re-activation or export
  • After 90-day grace period: All gym data, member records, and backups are permanently deleted
  • Face recognition encodings: Deleted immediately upon member removal or account closure
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by Indian GST regulations
  • Support tickets: Retained for 2 years for audit purposes
Export before you leave: We strongly recommend exporting your member data and reports before cancelling your subscription. Use the Admin Dashboard → Reports → Export to download a full backup.

Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection laws and general privacy principles, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

👁️ Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you or your gym at any time.

✏️ Right to Correct

Update or correct inaccurate information directly in the Admin Dashboard or by contacting us.

🗑️ Right to Delete

Request deletion of your account and all associated data. We'll process within 30 days.

📤 Right to Export

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@entergym.in with your gym subdomain and the request type. We'll respond within 14 business days.

10

Push Notifications

EnterGYM uses Web Push (via browser) and FCM (via the mobile app) to send notifications to gym members on behalf of gym owners. This includes plan expiry alerts, payment reminders, and gym announcements.

  • Members must explicitly opt in to receive push notifications
  • Only device push tokens are shared with FCM — no personal member data
  • Members can unsubscribe from push notifications at any time in browser settings or the app
  • Gym owners control notification content and frequency from the Admin Dashboard
  • FCM is operated by Google and subject to Google's Privacy Policy
11

WhatsApp & Third Parties

EnterGYM's WhatsApp reminder feature generates pre-filled wa.me links for gym staff to send manually. We do not directly integrate with the WhatsApp Business API or send messages on your behalf automatically.

  • WhatsApp messages are initiated manually by your staff, not sent automatically by EnterGYM
  • Member phone numbers used in WhatsApp links are from your gym's own database
  • WhatsApp's own privacy policy governs messages sent through their platform
  • We do not store WhatsApp message content or delivery status
Third-party services we use: Cloudflare (CDN/security), Google FCM (push notifications), Razorpay or similar (payments). Each is bound by their own privacy policy. We choose partners who meet appropriate data protection standards.

Children's Privacy

EnterGYM is a business-to-business platform intended for gym owners and fitness professionals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.

Gym owners who enroll minor members (where legally permitted) are responsible for obtaining appropriate parental consent and ensuring compliance with applicable laws governing minors' data in their jurisdiction.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected personal data of a child under 13, please contact us immediately at support@entergym.in and we will delete it promptly.
