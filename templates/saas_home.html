Got questions? We've got answers. Everything you need to know about EnterGYM.

EnterGYM is India's most affordable and feature-rich gym management software. It offers member management, GPS attendance, face recognition, push notifications, automated payment reminders, and a branded member app — all starting at just ₹4.99 per active member per month.

The Launchpad plan is completely free — forever. It supports up to 25 active members and includes core member management and manual attendance. No credit card required. It's perfect for new gyms testing the platform before scaling up.

With Pay-as-you-Go, you pay only for active members each month — no flat subscription fee. Rates are tiered: ₹6.99/member for up to 100 members, ₹5.99/member for 101–300 members, and ₹4.99/member for 301+ members. Your first month is free.

Yes, absolutely. You can switch between Launchpad, Pay-as-you-Go, and Fixed Subscription plans at any time with no penalty. There are no lock-in contracts. If you upgrade mid-cycle, the change takes effect from your next billing period.

Yes. Every new gym gets a 14-day free trial with full access to all Premium+ features — no credit card needed. Pay-as-you-Go and Fixed Subscription plans also include 1 month free on your first signup. You get to experience the full platform before paying anything.

EnterGYM offers three attendance methods: GPS geo-fencing (member checks in only when physically inside the gym), Face Recognition (camera identifies members automatically), and manual check-in via the staff dashboard. All methods log a timestamped entry that syncs in real time.

Yes. EnterGYM is built as a multi-tenant SaaS platform. Each branch operates with fully isolated data — separate members, attendance, payments, and reports. Gym owners can switch between branches instantly from one login without any risk of data overlap.

Migration is free and typically takes less than 24 hours. Just share your existing member list in any format — Excel, CSV, or even a WhatsApp contact export. Our team at Arrow SoftTech handles all the importing, configuration, and testing before going live.

Gym management software automates the day-to-day operations of running a gym — tracking member memberships, collecting payments, marking attendance, sending renewal reminders, and generating revenue reports. Without it, gym owners rely on WhatsApp groups, paper registers, and Excel sheets, which leads to missed payments, expired members walking in, and no visibility into revenue.

EnterGYM automatically sends push notifications to members 7, 4, 2, and 1 day before their membership expires — across PWA, browser, and the mobile app. Each reminder includes the due amount, expiry date, and membership details. Gyms using this feature report up to 22% improvement in on-time renewals.

Yes. Every gym on EnterGYM automatically gets a professional website and a branded Android mobile app — generated from your admin dashboard data. No developer is required. The iOS app is currently in development and will be available soon. The website shows your plans, working hours, location, and member count live.

Members get their own portal (web PWA + Android app) where they can view their active membership and validity, download payment receipts, apply for membership freeze, track attendance history, see workout plans and diet recommendations from their trainer, and receive renewal push notifications.

EnterGYM is sold and supported entirely via WhatsApp for simplicity. You contact us on +91 70000 32565, we understand your gym's needs, configure your platform, migrate your data, and have you live within 24 hours. Billing details and plan changes are also handled over WhatsApp by our team.

EnterGYM has a four-role RBAC system: Super Admin (full platform control), Gym Owner (full gym control), Trainer (member and workout management), and Receptionist (member enrollment and check-in). Each role sees only what it needs to — preventing unauthorized access and keeping data secure.

Yes. EnterGYM uses a fully isolated multi-tenant architecture — your gym's data is completely separate from every other gym on the platform. We prevent IDOR attacks by deriving gym context from the authenticated user, not from URL parameters. The platform runs at 99.9% uptime with regular backups.
