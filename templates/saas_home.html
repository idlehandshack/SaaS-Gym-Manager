Face recognition

Members walk in.
The camera marks
them present.

No app to open. No fob to tap. No QR code to scan. Face recognition runs passively in the background — members just walk through the door.

  • HARDWARE Works on standard IP cameras already installed in your gym. No new equipment to buy.
  • ACCURACY Members enroll once with 3–5 reference photos. Every subsequent entry is matched with a confidence score.
  • FALLBACK Camera offline? Members can check in via GPS or manual entry — no attendance gaps ever.
  • PRIVACY Face data is stored encrypted, scoped to your gym only. Never shared across tenants.
